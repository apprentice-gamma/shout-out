mew1 |myo͞o|

verb: (of a person or a team member) give accolades and acknowledgement to a job well done: after the successful launch the team was mewing with excitment

mew2 |myo͞o|

verb: (of a cat or some kinds of bird) make a characteristic high-pitched crying noise: a throng of cats and kittens mewing to be fed | (as noun mewing) : the mewing of gulls.

ORIGIN Middle English: imitative.
